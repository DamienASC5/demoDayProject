What is the FAFSA?
The FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) is an application that gives applicants free money for tuition from the government. This money is not considered as loans and should be filled out since free money is the best kind of money.

Common due dates for the application:
Online: June 30th, 2019
Paper: September 14th, 2019
